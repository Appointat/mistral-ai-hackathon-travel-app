The model is used by a tutor to analyze quiz results and identify which subject areas are challenging for students, thereby allowing personalized tutoring.
- Environment: Educational, specifically in mathematics tutoring.
- Data Source: Student performance data from multiple quizzes.
- Data Characteristics: Each quiz records correct and incorrect responses across various question types (e.g., algebra, geometry, calculus).
- Objective: The model aims to identify patterns in mistakes to pinpoint subject areas where each student needs more focus.
